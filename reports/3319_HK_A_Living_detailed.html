Executive Summary

Company Overview

A-Living Smart City Services is one of China's leading property management companies, originally spun off from Agile Group Holdings (3383.HK) in 2018. The company provides comprehensive property management services including basic property management, value-added services to property developers, and community value-added services. Despite challenges from the broader China property sector crisis, A-Living has maintained operational resilience through geographic diversification and service expansion.

Investment Thesis

A-Living represents a compelling value opportunity in the distressed China property management sector. The company's scale advantages, diversified customer base beyond parent company Agile Group, and growing third-party GFA provide defensive characteristics. However, significant risks remain tied to parent company Agile Group's debt restructuring and broader sector headwinds.

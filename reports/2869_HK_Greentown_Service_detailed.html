Executive Summary

Company Overview

Greentown Service Group is one of China's leading premium property management companies, renowned for its high-quality service standards and strong brand recognition. The company operates primarily in the Yangtze River Delta region and has expanded nationwide, focusing on mid-to-high-end residential properties. As a subsidiary of Greentown China Holdings, the company benefits from a steady pipeline of new projects while also pursuing third-party expansion.

Investment Thesis

Greentown Service represents a compelling opportunity to own a premium property management franchise at a significant discount to intrinsic value. The company commands a 20-40% fee premium over industry averages, achieving RMB 3.5-4.5 per sqm monthly versus industry norms. This pricing power stems from 20+ years of brand heritage and the 'Heartfelt Service' philosophy delivering industry-leading customer satisfaction. The asset-light model generates strong free cash flow with recurring revenue characteristics.

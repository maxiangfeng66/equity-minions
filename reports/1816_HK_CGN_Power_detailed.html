Executive Summary

CGN Power Co., Ltd. (1816 HK) is China's largest independent nuclear power operator by installed capacity, representing a pure-play opportunity in China's nuclear power expansion. The company operates primarily through its managed nuclear power plants across China, benefiting from the government's ambitious clean energy targets and the ongoing nuclear renaissance.

Investment Thesis

CGN Power offers the best pure-play exposure to China's structural nuclear expansion at a reasonable valuation with attractive income characteristics. China's nuclear capacity is set to expand from 57 GW to 100+ GW by 2030 under explicit government policy targets, with CGN capturing 40-45% of new capacity additions. The company's operational excellence, technology leadership with Hualong One, and 4-5% dividend yield provide compelling total return potential.

Key Highlights

  • Dominant position in China's nuclear power sector with ~27-30 GW attributable capacity

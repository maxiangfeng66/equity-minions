Executive Summary

Xinte Energy Co., Ltd. (1799.HK) is a leading Chinese polysilicon manufacturer and subsidiary of TBEA, a major power equipment conglomerate. The company operates approximately 200,000 MT of polysilicon capacity, making it a top-5 global producer. In addition to polysilicon, Xinte has a diversified wind power business that provides stable recurring cash flows, differentiating it from pure-play polysilicon competitors.

Industry Headwind: The polysilicon market faces unprecedented overcapacity of 80-100% (2.5M MT capacity vs 1.2-1.4M MT demand). Prices have collapsed 85% from CNY 300+/kg to CNY 35-50/kg, pushing even Tier 1 producers near or below breakeven.

Investment Thesis

Xinte Energy represents a deep-value cyclical play on polysilicon industry recovery. The company's Tier 1 cost position (CNY 38-45/kg), wind power diversification, and TBEA parent support provide survival advantages through the severe industry downturn. At 0.36x book value, the stock offers asymmetric upside if industry rationalization occurs, with probability-weighted fair value of HKD 9.23 implying 105% upside. However, timing uncertainty warrants a patient approach.
